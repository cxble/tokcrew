Core Package — Conversion & Conversation Management
The foundation: always-on response handling across comments and DMs, built to convert attention into sales.
1. Comment Section Management
•	Timely, on-brand replies to every comment
•	Spam and troll filtering
2. DM Chatbot Automation (FAQ)
•	Auto-replies for common questions (pricing, shipping, availability)
•	Always-on coverage outside business hours
3. DM Human Takeover (LLM-Assisted Ticketing)
•	AI flags complex or high-intent conversations for human handling
•	Seamless handoff with full conversation context — no repeated questions
4. TikTok Shop Comment-to-Purchase Funnel
•	Product-tagged comment responses that drive viewers to checkout
•	Affiliate/creator comment engagement support
5. Live Selling Support
•	Real-time comment monitoring during LIVE sessions
•	Moderator bots for high-volume chat
•	Automated “buy now” / restock prompts timed to the live
6. Lead Qualification & CRM Handoff
•	Hot / warm / cold lead scoring from DM conversations
•	Auto-push to CRM, Google Sheet, or Excel tracker
•	Funnel routing to landing page where applicable
7. Sentiment & Complaint Monitoring
•	Early flagging of negative sentiment spikes or PR risks
•	Daily/weekly complaint summary so issues get caught before they escalate
Add-On Package — Content Support
Optional support to keep the content pipeline moving. Best framed as convenience, not a creative agency replacement.
•	Content calendar planning & scheduled posting automation
•	Basic editing & repurposing (long-form → TikTok-sized clips, cross-platform resizing)
Marketing Add-On — Performance & Ads
For clients ready to scale spend behind what's already converting.
•	Performance dashboard — engagement, response time, DM-to-sale conversion, tracked weekly
•	TikTok Ads / Spark Ads management — boosting proven organic content
One-Time Setup Fee (recommended)
Covers the upfront engineering work: chatbot configuration, CRM/dashboard integration, and live-selling bot setup. Charged once per client, separate from the monthly retainer — this protects the value of the technical build-out.
